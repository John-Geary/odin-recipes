Guacamole Recipe

Description

You can make this guacamole smooth or chunky depending on your tates.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2 roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt
  2. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic
  3. Stir in cayeene pepper
  4. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately
